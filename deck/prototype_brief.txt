TRINETRA = Tactical Real-time Intelligence & Network Engine for Tracking,
Reasoning & Analytics (also "trinetra" = the third eye that sees the hidden).
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

TRINETRA (Tactical Real-time Intelligence & Network Engine for Tracking, Reasoning & Analytics) is an agentic crime-intelligence console for the Karnataka State Police. Investigators query it in Kannada or English by voice or text. It turns natural language into safe, validated ZCQL over the crime database, uncovers hidden networks between offenders, victims, phones and accounts, profiles repeat offenders with a transparent risk score, and answers with an interactive graph, hotspot map and timeline — every fact cited to its FIR. Adds context-aware follow-ups, money-trail analysis, similar-case leads, forecasting and PDF export. Built entirely on Catalyst: Slate frontend, Functions, QuickML LLM-Serving (Qwen), Data Store (relational + edge tables), Zia voice, SmartBrowz, and Authentication with role-based access and a SELECT-only query gate. Impact: cuts FIR-to-lead cross-referencing from days to seconds — turning reactive policing proactive. Scalable, secure, production-grade.
