TRINETRA (Tactical Real-time Intelligence & Network Engine for Tracking, Reasoning & Analytics) — PS1: an agentic conversational AI for the KSP crime database. Officers ask in Kannada or English (voice or text); it turns natural language into safe, validated ZCQL, uncovers hidden offender–victim–phone–money networks, scores repeat offenders, and answers with an interactive graph, hotspot map and timeline — every fact cited to its FIR. Also: money-trail analysis, similar-case leads, forecasting, and PDF export. Stack: Catalyst Slate, Functions, QuickML (Qwen), Data Store, Zia voice, SmartBrowz, Auth + RBAC, SELECT-only query gate. Impact: cuts FIR-to-lead work from days to seconds — proactive, explainable, production-grade policing.
