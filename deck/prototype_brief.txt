Challenge: PS1 — Intelligent Conversational AI for the KSP Crime Database
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

TRINETRA is an agentic crime-intelligence console for the Karnataka State Police. Investigators query it in Kannada or English by voice or text. It converts natural language to safe, validated ZCQL over the crime database, uncovers hidden networks between offenders, victims, phones and accounts, profiles repeat offenders with a transparent risk score, and answers with an interactive network graph, hotspot map and timeline — every fact cited to its FIR for full explainability. Context-aware follow-ups, money-trail analysis, early-warning alerts, and one-click PDF export of the conversation. Built entirely on Catalyst: Slate frontend, Functions + API Gateway, QuickML LLM-Serving (Qwen) + RAG, Data Store (relational + edge tables), Zia voice, Stratus, SmartBrowz, Authentication with role-based access and a SELECT-only query gate. Impact: cuts FIR-to-lead cross-referencing from days to seconds — turning reactive policing proactive. Scalable, secure, production-grade.
